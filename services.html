Forest Walks

Take an easy stroll along our forest walk paths, situated round Park Farm En Route. Pefect path ways for dog owners and anyone who likes to cycle! We have picnic benches and bins dotted along the walks, for familes and or anyone who would like some respite when visiting. Enjoy looking over the lake, eating away at your sandwitches with a hot drink while taking in this breathtaking view. We hope you enjoy your adventurous visit here!

Outdoor Play Park

We welcome you and your families to our outdoor play park here at Park Farm En Route, Adults must accompany under 5 year olds at the park. We hope you enjoy your visit to the park and fullfill your adventurous skills.
