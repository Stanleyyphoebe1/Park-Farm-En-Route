Welcome to
Park Farm En Route

Welcome to Park Farm En Route. We are passionate about adventure activities and providing the very best experiences for you no matter your age and ability. You can enjoy the relaxation of our onsite diner! And or enjoy our fresh produce at the shop here on site. There is plenty of land to enjoy, many adventures here to explore along with our play park area, forestry walks for you and your pets to enjoy (dogs to be kept on a lead at all times).

Play Park

Play Parks are full of swings, slides and sandpits along with little wooden houses. There is a toddlers play area (supervision at all times required).

Petting Zoo

Petting/Feeding Zoos are such fun, for anyone of any age and ability! Feeding the animals will be supervised at all times for the saftey of our site and animals.

Forest Walks

Forest walk paths are great for taking your pet dogs along and all familes to enjoy!. Picnic benches and bins are situated through the forest for you to enjoy your own individual break times on your vist.

About Us

Based between Carlisle and Edinburgh, we have everything you would require here at our farm park, its own shop, play park in a safe, fun and easy environment. We have a diner offering mulitple options such as gluten free, dairy free, vegan and vegetarian.

We boast over 55 years of experience and knowledge in the agricultural industry between the directors. Our fully qualified staff are continuously training to be the best so you know you are in safe hands.
